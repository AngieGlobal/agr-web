Refinance for your house

Refinancing your mortgage is a common practice used to lower monthly payments, interest rates, lower your DTI and more.

Rate-and-Term Refinancing
  • It's generally a good time to refinance when mortgage rates are 2% lower than the current rate on your loan.
HELOC
  • It's generally a good time to refinance when mortgage rates are 2% lower than the current rate on your loan.
Cash-out Refinancing
  • Most lenders charge fees to refinance a loan. If you plan to only stay in the property for a couple of years, your monthly savings may not accumulate to recoup these costs.
